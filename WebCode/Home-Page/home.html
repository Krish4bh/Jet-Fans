About Us

A hot summer day in 2020, when our founder realized that fans in the market were either too noisy, too costly, or too inefficient. What if we could design a fan that was silent, stylish, and energy-efficient all at once?

That question became the spark that launched Jet Fans.

Starting from a small workshop with a handful of passionate engineers and designers, we built our first prototypes by hand. We tested, refined, and tested again — until we achieved the perfect balance of powerful airflow, minimal noise, and modern design.

Today, Jet Fans is more than just a product — it’s a movement. We’ve helped thousands of homes, offices, and businesses stay cool, save on electricity bills, and enjoy comfort without compromise.

At Jet Fans, we believe comfort should not be a privilege, but a right. That’s why we started the“One in a Hundred” initiative:

For every 100 Jet Fans sold, we donate 1 fan to someone in need — from rural schools and community halls to families struggling through the hottest months without proper cooling.

By choosing Jet Fans, you don’t just stay cool — you help others live a little better too.
